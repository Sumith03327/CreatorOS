# **App Name**: Creator Hub

## Core Features:

- Dashboard Overview: Provides a comprehensive view of channel key performance metrics (subscribers, views, upload activity), aggregated statistics, and quick navigation.
- AI Channel Analysis Tool: A tool that performs smart analysis of a channel's latest upload and overall performance, providing insights on video retention, content engagement, and audience behavior to optimize content strategy.
- AI Action Plan Generator: Leverages AI to generate customized, actionable steps and strategic content plans based on current channel data, competitor analysis, and market trends.
- Personalized AI Creator Tips: Delivers dynamic, AI-generated tips and recommendations to creators, personalized to their recent channel activity and performance trends.
- YouTube Channel Integration: Securely connects to and synchronizes data from user-designated YouTube channels, providing a foundation for analytics and planning.
- Activity and Task Feed: Displays real-time progress, status updates, and history for analytical tasks, generated plans, and other platform activities.

## Style Guidelines:

- Dark Sidebar Background: A deep, professional charcoal provided by the user (#1a1a2e), providing a strong contrast to the content area.
- Main Content Background: An exceptionally light, cool-toned white (#FAF9FC), subtly tinted to align with the primary accent, ensuring excellent readability.
- Primary Accent Color: A vibrant and empowering purple (#7B5CF0), provided by the user, used for interactive elements, calls-to-action, and key data highlights.
- Secondary Accent Color: A confident, analogous blue (#264ADE) for visual variation and to emphasize complementary actions or data points.
- Body and Headline Font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its neutral, objective, and modern aesthetic, ensuring high readability across all data and content displays.
- Utilize a consistent set of clean, minimalist line icons that clearly communicate functionality for analytics, content types, and settings.
- A fixed dark left sidebar with primary navigation and branding, contrasting with a responsive, light main content area organized into intuitive dashboard sections and cards.
- Implement subtle, polished animations for loading states, data updates, and hover effects on interactive elements, enhancing user experience without distraction.
